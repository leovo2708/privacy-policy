Privacy Policy Last updated: 2025/11/18 This Chrome Extension (“the Extension”) is designed to help users format the text content they copy from web pages and copy the processed result to their clipboard. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data. 1. Information We Collect The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information. The Extension only processes the text that you manually copy from web pages. All processing happens locally on your device. We do NOT: Track browsing activity Send data to external servers Store text or user information Use cookies or analytics tools 2. How We Use Your Data The Extension only uses the text you copy for the purpose of formatting or transforming it, according to the features you use. All operations occur in your browser, and no data ever leaves your device. 3. Data Sharing We do not share any data with third parties. We do not sell, trade, or disclose any information to outside parties. 4. Permissions The Extension may require certain permissions, such as: Access to read clipboard content Access to modify clipboard content These permissions are used solely for text formatting and copying, and not for collecting data. 5. Security Because no data is transmitted or stored externally, the risk of data exposure is minimal. All processing occurs securely within your browser. 6. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary. Updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. 7.
