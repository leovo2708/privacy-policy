Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025/11/18

This Chrome Extension (the "Extension") helps users format text that they copy from web pages and then copy the formatted result to the clipboard. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information that may be involved in the use of the Extension.

1. Information We Collect

The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personally identifiable information.

The Extension only processes text that you manually copy from web pages. All processing is performed locally on your device within the browser.

We do not:

  • Track browsing activity.
  • Send data to external servers.
  • Store text or user information on remote services.
  • Use cookies or analytics tools to collect personal data.

2. How We Use Your Data

The only data the Extension uses is the text you explicitly provide by copying it. That text is used solely for the purpose of formatting or transforming it according to the feature you use, and then placing the result in your clipboard. No data leaves your device.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. We do not sell, rent, or trade any information to outside parties.

4. Permissions

The Extension may request only the permissions it needs to perform its function, such as access to read or modify clipboard content. These permissions are used solely for text formatting and copying and are not used to collect or transmit data.

5. Security

Because no data is transmitted or stored externally, the risk of external data exposure is minimal. All processing occurs locally in your browser session.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
